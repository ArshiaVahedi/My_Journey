Phase 1: Foundation & Early Accolades (2007-2018)

The Spark of Innovation (High School)

  • ✨ Action: I founded and managed a robotics non-profit, securing ~$10,000 in funding and fostering STEM education.
  • Teaching: I taught C Programming and Algorithms in Robotics (2008-2012).
  • ✨ Recognition: I was awarded the Gold Medal (First Rank) at the IRAN OPEN Junior Robotic Competitions (2009). This early success demonstrated my innate drive for technical excellence and leadership.
  • Competition: I competed in over 15 robotics competitions, achieving top ranks, and represented Iran as a member of the national robotics team at the Singapore World RoboCup.

Building a Scientific Mind (Medical School)

  • Education: I earned my Doctor of Medicine (MD) from Zahedan University of Medical Sciences (2011-2018).
  • ✨ Exceptional Performance: I was awarded the Gold Medal in the 9th National Scientific Olympiad for Medical Students (2016) and named "Best Student Researcher of the University" for two consecutive years (2014, 2015). I also received awards for the 6th Student Olympiad (Art in Medical Education, 2013), Third Rank in Medical Basic Sciences Exam (2013), and Top Cultural Rank of University (2012).
  • ✨ Leadership in Action: As a leader in the Student Research Committee, I mentored over 100 novice students, reviewed approximately 200 research proposals, and organized 5 annual research congresses, showcasing a commitment to nurturing a scientific community. I designed and taught over 80 research-related workshops.
  • Early Research Focus: My thesis on infant neuro-development and cognition laid the groundwork for my future specialization.
  • Voluntary Experience: I served as Student Secretary and Director at the Red Crescent (Red Cross) Center, coordinating disaster relief efforts during the Kermanshah earthquake.

Phase 2: Clinical Acumen & Advanced Research (2019-Present)

On the Frontlines of Medicine

  • Role: I served as a General Practitioner in Tehran and Zahedan (October 2019 - Present).
  • ✨ Impact: I provided critical emergency care to over 120 patients daily during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, demonstrating exceptional resilience and clinical expertise under pressure.

Deep Dive into the Brain (Postdoctoral Research)

  • Affiliation: I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at CRDRC, Masih Daneshvari Hospital (March 2019 - Present).
  • ✨ Advanced Methodologies: I initiated and led pioneering studies on brain functional connectivity using Task-based fMRI (with a $12,000 grant) and neurocognitive outcomes with EEG analysis (with a $5,000 grant). I also conducted self-funded research on UVC radiation for SARS-CoV-2 disinfection.
  • ✨ High-Impact Publications: I analyzed data from over 400 COVID-19 cases, resulting in three peer-reviewed articles and contributing significantly to the field.
  • Research Development: I developed innovative research proposals and enhanced research center operations by updating databases and optimizing websites.

Phase 3: Pioneering the Future of Neuroscience (2024-Present)

Visionary Leadership

  • 🚀 Innovation: I founded and direct the Online Neuroscience Network Research Center (ONNRC) (2025 - Present), a global virtual platform designed to democratize neuroscience research. This initiative highlights my forward-thinking approach to scientific collaboration, engaging ~11 active researchers and securing a ROR identifier.
  • ✨ Current Research: I am conducting independent, self-funded research leveraging Deep Learning (DL) to investigate brain functional connectivity and neurodegenerative disorders. I have authored a peer-reviewed review article and submitted three additional manuscripts for publication.
